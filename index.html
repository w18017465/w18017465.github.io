Hello There! This is the portfolio of Christopher Goddard, an aspiring Computer Science Student at Newcastle University. This website lists projects I've worked on, my skills and my contact details - looking forward to hearing from you!

For the last 9 months I have been studying my first year of a 4 year Computer Science undergraduate course. During this time I have worked on 5 different areas of learning: Programming, Software Engineering, Computer Architecture, Mathematics and Website Development. These modules have taught me already a large amount of knowledge, despite how short my time has been with them so far, and have allowed me to develop many key skills that you can find under my skills section. In the future I plan on specialising in Human-Computer Interfaces and physical interactions between machines and users, however that may change in the future due to the many marvellously fascinating different courses available.

Outside of University I am also no stranger to computers and programming, having grown up from a young age using a computer resulting in a competent fluency even when working in an unfamiliar environment or on an unknown topic. In my spare time I can often be found on my computer, perhaps browsing the internet, coding for fun or playing video games, or enjoying various forms of literature.
